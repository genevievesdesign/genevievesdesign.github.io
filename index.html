Get in Touch

I’m Genevieve, a Canterbury-based designer with a love for all things typographic. I create work that feels deliberate and visually refined, for those who value clarity, consistency, and the finer details.

I care about how small design decisions shape the overall experience. Whether working within an established identity or developing something new, I apply design thoughtfully across print, digital, and campaigns. If you need thoughtful, detail-driven design, I’d love to hear about it.
