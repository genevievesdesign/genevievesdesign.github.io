Against the Current

An editorial publication translating environmental decline into a physical reading experience.

Role: Editorial Design, Typography

Format: Concertina publication

Outcome: Exhibition / Print piece

← Previous genevieves.design Next →

Overview

‘Against the Current’ explores water, memory, and materiality through a tactile typographic publication that translates environmental change into a spatial reading experience.

Context

The Selwyn River is treated as a living system rather than a static landscape. The work frames environmental change through narrative and emotional connection.

Approach

The project uses print as a physical medium, where tactility, layering, and the concertina format reflect movement and time. Environmental transitions shift from clean to polluted, abundance to scarcity, and swimmable to unswimmable. The structure reinforces continuity, allowing the narrative to unfold as a connected flow.

Outcome

The publication operates as subtle activism, exploring freshwater degradation through typography, material, and sequence.
