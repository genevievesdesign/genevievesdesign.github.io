Pyper Vision

A focused brand refresh for Pyper Vision, refining clarity, consistency, and confidence across digital and collateral applications.

Role: Brand Identity, Art Direction

Overview

Pyper Vision required a brand refresh to better reflect the clarity and reliability of their service. The project involved refining the existing logo and developing a cohesive visual identity that could extend across digital and marketing touchpoints.

Context

Operating within a highly specific technical space, the brand needed to communicate trust, precision, and innovation. The challenge was to elevate the existing identity while maintaining recognisability, ensuring the refresh felt both considered and commercially appropriate.

Approach

The refresh centred on a refined blue palette, referencing the atmospheric conditions the technology operates within. A more defined blue was introduced to improve visibility and on-screen clarity, while maintaining technical precision. The logo was subtly refined to improve balance and legibility, supporting a cohesive and adaptable identity.

Outcome

The updated identity delivers a clearer, more confident brand presence. The system was applied across marketing collateral and presentation design, creating consistency across touchpoints and strengthening the overall perception of the business.
